Title: BWV 595 - For Electric Guitar
Album: Beruehmte Orgelwerke
Author: Johann Sebastian Bach

Original taken from classtab.org, which I believe existed on OLGA first. 
A worthwhile visit for anyone interested in classical guitar tablatures.
An excellent exercise in itself and a most satisfying tune to play.

This rearrangement's purpose is to reduce some of the obtuse organ fretting in the original, to
add some instruction on matching the organ piece's particular embellishments with a guitar, and
finally give back to a community which i've benefited from most of my life (olga.net/classtab.org).

Log:
June 5, 2023	- First section altered to how i've played it for years to see if this re-arrangement idea is worthwhile
